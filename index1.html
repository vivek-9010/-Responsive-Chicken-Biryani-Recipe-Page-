Chicken Biryani Recipe

An aromatic and flavorful one-pot dish, this chicken biryani is perfect for any occasion. Serve with a side of raita or salan for a hearty meal.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup fried onions
  • 1/4 cup ghee or oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, spices, and salt for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee in a pot, add whole spices, and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft, then add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  5. Layer the rice over the chicken, add water, and cook on low heat until done.
  6. Fluff the rice, garnish with fried onions, and serve hot.
